About Me

Min-Joo Jang

I am a Postdoctoral-Fellow at The University of Hong Kong under the mentorship of Ben Kane and Yuk Kam Lau.

I completed my Ph.D. at University of Cologne in 2018. My Supervisor is Kathrin Bringmann.

My research interests lie in Number Theory and Combinatorics, in particular integer partitions, q-series and modular forms.
